About Me

I received my DVM and M.S. from Seoul National University, College of Veterinary Medicine. My M.S. research was on image-based machine learning for disease diagnosis.

I am currently an AI Research Lead at Madup Inc., and was previously at Seoul National University Hospital and VUNO Inc.
